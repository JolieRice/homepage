Sound in Motion

This project explores the connection between sound and visuals through an audio visualizer, turning music into patterns and imagery. By capturing three songs at different timestamps, I've created a side-by-side comparison of how each track translates visually over time. The result is a collection that showcases the unique rhythm and energy of each moment in the music.

Dream: 30 second intervals
Flow: 30 second intervals
Lazy: 15 second intervals
