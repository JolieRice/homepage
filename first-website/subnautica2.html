Subnautica 2

The next Subnautica game

The third entry in the Subnautica series, currently in development by Unknown Worlds Entertainment, promises to further expand the universe. While details are still emerging, the developers have confirmed they aim to continue the franchise's legacy of exploration, survival, and storytelling. Fans can expect new biomes, tools, and creatures, as well as deeper insights into the lore of 4546B and its alien inhabitants. Anticipation is high as the creators work to refine and build on the series' groundbreaking gameplay and immersive world-building.
