Subnautica

A Deep Dive into an Underwater Game

Subnautica is an open-world survival game series developed by Unknown Worlds Entertainment. The series includes the original Subnautica and its sequel, Subnautica: Below Zero. Both games immerse players in the alien oceans of planet 4546B, where they must explore diverse biomes, craft tools, build underwater habitats, and uncover the planet's secrets. The developers are actively expanding the Subnautica universe, with a third game currently in development.
