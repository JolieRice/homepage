Subnautica

The first Subnautica game

Subnautica is the original installment in the series, introducing players to the underwater world of planet 4546B. After crash-landing on this alien planet, players must explore its vast and vibrant ocean biomes, ranging from shallow coral reefs to the dark and dangerous depths of the Lost River and Lava Zone. The game focuses on survival, resource gathering, crafting, and base building while unraveling the mystery of the planet's history and the alien disease known as the Kharaa. With its open-world design, immersive storytelling, and stunning visuals, Subnautica delivers a unique and thrilling underwater experience.

Leviathans

Leviathans in Subnautica are large, powerful creatures found in the deep ocean. Some are aggressive, while others are neutral, and they play a significant role in the planet's ecosystem.
